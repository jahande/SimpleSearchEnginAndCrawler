Donald B. Crouch - Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Donald B. Crouch From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to: navigation, search Donald B. Crouch is a Professor of Computer Science at the University of Minnesota, Duluth.[1] He is considered as one of the pioneers in the fields of information retrieval and data mining.
